// Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

🎲

Random Pick

Each publication draws a random video from your pool — no duplicates until the full library has been used.

📅

Fixed Schedule

Set your two daily posting times once. The scheduler runs silently in the background, every single day.

✍️

Per-platform Captions

Each video carries its own caption for TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook. Tailored copy, zero extra effort.

📲

Telegram Inbox

Send videos and captions directly from your phone via Telegram — inline with the video or as a follow-up message.

📋

Publication Log

Every post is logged with timestamp, platform status, and file reference. Full traceability, always.

🔁

Recycle Mode

When your pool runs dry, flip a single flag to recycle all videos and start the rotation over again.

// Workflow

Four steps.
Then it runs itself.

01

Upload via Telegram

Send your short video to your private Telegram bot directly from your phone after editing.

02

Add captions

Include captions in the video message, or send them in a follow-up text message — whichever fits your workflow.

03

Scheduler picks randomly

At your scheduled times, a random unpublished video is selected from the pool.

04

Posted everywhere

The video is published simultaneously to TikTok, Instagram Reels, and your Facebook Page.
